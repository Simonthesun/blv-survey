Digital Content Creation by Blind and Low Vision Individuals

The Inclusive Design Research Group and the CREATE Center at the University of Washington are trying to understand how blind and low vision individuals currently create digital content. Our goal is to understand digital content creation needs from people with and without extensive creation experience. If you feel your experiences may help our research, we kindly ask you to fill in the survey linked below. For questions, please contact the lead investigator, Lotus Zhang, at hanziz@uw.edu. Thank you for supporting this research effort!
