Understanding Digital Content Creation by Blind and Low Vision People

The Inclusive Design Research Group at the University of Washington is exploring ways to support people who are blind or with low vision in creating digital content. In this study, we are trying to understand how different blind and low vision individuals currently create digital content. Thank you so much for supporting this research effort! You can opt into a drawing for a $50 Amazon gift card (odds of winning: one out of every forty participants) upon completing the survey.
